About Me

I am proficient in processing and using different kinds of data to create audiovisual installations and multimedia art pieces. Utilizing Blender, Touchdesigner, Houdini, and Computer Science skills, I create media for local bands, businesses, and my own personal narrative projects.

Blender

I've been using Blender to create audiovisual animation, motion graphics, music videos, and environment design. I have experience in procedural modeling and a variety of addons for Blender that I incorperate with my art. I capture dream-like and otherwordly experiences in my pieces through blending visual effects and geometric data. I utilize pointclouds, Blender GIS, and photoscans to create a hyperreal environment, one that blends the subconscious into something tangible.

Touchdesigner

Touchdesigner has allowed me to create realtime animations driven by data and music. Manipulating and processing data has allowed me to push the cerebral work I make in my renders into something modular and experential. Touchdesigner's advanced data processing tools has allowed me to create data visualizations, implement GLSL into my art, and allow them to become live performances.



Houdini

I use Houdini's powerful tools to procedurally model and create unique animations. Houdini enables me to composite more complex simulations and animation within my projects, as well as interface with data more easily during the rendering process.
